# Clinic and staff onboarding

## Clinic onboarding

1. Confirm the clinic owner, privacy contact, support contact, and pilot scope.
2. Confirm approved organization and location details.
3. Invite only staff who require access and assign least-privilege roles.
4. Complete synthetic-data workflow acceptance before importing any approved data.
5. Review privacy, retention, incident, maintenance, and rollback procedures.

## Staff onboarding

- Use an individual account; never share credentials.
- Confirm organization, location, and role access before using clinic data.
- Complete privacy, security, phishing, and incident-reporting training.
- Use only approved devices and browsers; report lost devices immediately.
- Escalate unexpected access, data display, or integration behavior with the URL and correlation ID, never screenshots containing patient data.
