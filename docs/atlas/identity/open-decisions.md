# Open decisions

These questions require founder, product, privacy, or security approval. This document intentionally does not guess.

1. Can one organization contain multiple independently branded clinics?
2. May practitioners work across organizations, and what commercial or privacy constraints apply?
3. Does location scope inherit from clinic scope exactly as described, or must every effective location be explicit?
4. Are custom organization roles available in the first commercial release?
5. May platform support staff use controlled break-glass access? If yes, what approval, duration, disclosure, and audit controls are required?
6. Are invitation emails sent through Supabase, a dedicated email provider, or a future communications service?
7. Do profiles require legal names, preferred names, or both, and who can view each?
8. Do organizations need Canadian province-specific defaults for timezone, address, governance, or retention?
9. Must audit events later be replicated to immutable external storage?
10. Should organization slugs be mutable, and what redirect/history rules are required?
11. What retention and redaction policy applies to membership, invitation, security, and network metadata?
12. Which actions require MFA step-up or an elevated Auth assurance level?
13. Which organization roles may grant each staff, role, scope, and export permission?
